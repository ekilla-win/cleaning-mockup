About SparkleClean Pro

We've been transforming spaces and exceeding expectations for over 15 years. Discover our story and meet the team behind your clean spaces.

Our Story

Founded in 2009 by Sarah Mitchell, SparkleClean Pro began as a small, family-owned business with a simple mission: to provide exceptional cleaning services that make a real difference in people's lives. What started as a one-person operation has grown into a trusted team of over 50 cleaning professionals serving thousands of homes and businesses.

Our commitment to quality, reliability, and eco-friendly practices has earned us recognition as the region's leading cleaning service. We believe that a clean environment leads to a clearer mind, better health, and improved productivity. This belief drives everything we do, from the products we choose to the training we provide our team.

Today, we continue to innovate and expand our services while maintaining the personal touch and attention to detail that has made us a trusted name in professional cleaning. Every member of our team shares Sarah's original vision: to deliver cleaning services that exceed expectations and create spaces where people can thrive.

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to industry leadership, here's how we've grown and evolved over the years.

2009

The Beginning

Sarah Mitchell founded SparkleClean Pro with just $500 and a dream to provide exceptional cleaning services. Started with residential cleaning in the local neighborhood.

2012

Team Expansion
